Evidence integrity

“How do you know this file wasn't altered?”

That's the question that ends a careless case. RaizIntel is built so the answer isn't your word. It's already on the record.

  • Fingerprinted on arrival, re-checked on every open

    The moment a file lands, RaizIntel takes a cryptographic fingerprint of it. That signature is tied to the file's exact contents. Every time the file is opened, it's checked again. Change a single byte and you'd know.

  • An add-only chain of custody

    Every action on a piece of evidence is logged as it happens. Entries can be added, never rewritten or erased. That's enforced by the database itself, not by a policy someone can choose to ignore.

  • Approved reports lock

    Once a report is approved, the system refuses to change it. Not discouraged. Refused. A correction goes in as a supplement, on the record, and the original still stands.

  • Corrections are attributed, with a reason

    When evidence is corrected, RaizIntel records who did it and requires a reason. The change goes on the record. Nothing is quietly swapped in.

  • Backed up off-site, recovery tested

    Your files are copied off-site. We've tested bringing them back, not just assumed it would work.

  • Downloads run through the app

    Files come out through RaizIntel, never raw storage links that can leak or get shared by accident.

Case organization

One case. Everything in it, in one place.

No hunting across folders, drives, and email threads. A case holds its evidence, its people, and its vehicles together, and stays that way from intake to close.

  • Evidence, subjects, and vehicles under one case

    Photos, video, documents, the people involved, and the cars they drive, all attached to the case they belong to instead of scattered across your desktop and your inbox.

  • Subjects and vehicles you build once

    A subject or a vehicle is its own record. Link it to any case it turns up in, and what you already know about it comes along.

  • A full activity history

    Every case keeps its own record of who did what, and when. You can see how it got to where it is, not just where it stands.

Reports

Reports that go out on your letterhead, not ours.

The report is what the client pays for and what a court reads. RaizIntel builds it on your agency's letterhead and holds it to an approval step before it leaves.

  • Surveillance, incident, and expense
